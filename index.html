Game
Points subtracted from the next leg's starting score when you lose a leg. Floor is always 121.
Standard 9 innings: each player throws 3 darts per inning at the inning's number (1–9). Single = 1 run, Double = 2 runs, Triple = 3 runs. Highest total runs after 9 innings wins.
18 holes (1 → 18). Each player throws 3 darts per hole at the hole's number. Triple = 1 stroke (eagle), Double = 2 (birdie), Single = 3 (par), miss = 5. Lowest total strokes wins.
Classic: 12, 13, 14, 15, Double, 16, 17, 18, Triple, 19, 20, 25 (bull), Bullseye. Each turn = 3 darts at that target; score = sum × multiplier. Miss all 3 on the bullseye-stage targets and your score halves. Highest score at the end wins.
